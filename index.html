A Brief History of South Clicks



South Clicks has started in 2010 with a team of 2 members named Ryan Reynolds and Adria Arjona. It's main aim is to provide an excellent customer friendly service. They started with Event and Wildlife photography in the beginning. They expanded photography projects to Creative, Commercial, Corporate, Fashion and Weddings since 2019.


Mélanie Laurent and Manuel Garcia-Rulfo joined the South Clicks in 2013. Both of them have been graduated from the famous California Institute of the Arts located in California. They have brought creative ideas for enhancing the growth of South Clicks. The toiling service provided by South Clicks always make the people to choose us.



Now South Clicks has 520 employees and 4 major offices in USA, Canada, Australia and India. We have been awarded for the best photography firm in 2019, 2020 and ranked among top 50 photography companies across the world. We have started providing Internship programmes to all the youngesters around the world through offline and online.
